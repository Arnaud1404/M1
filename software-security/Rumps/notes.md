# Operation Rubicom

## TLDR
  The CIA and its west german counterpart BND ran the largest supply chain attack of the 20th century,
owning the Swiss company Crypto AG through a complex web of shell companies, even their employees
didn't know they were working for foreign intelligence agencies. Switzerland being recognized for its
historical neutrality made it the perfect target for such an operation, which lasted for almost 25 years,
from 1970 to 1993.

The encryption machines were sabotaged by manipulating the RNG device inside them. What should have taken months to decrypt could only take a few seconds. Machines without flaws were sold to NATO countries, and
flawed ones to countries like Iran, Libya or Argentina.
